About Madurai, Tamil Nadu

Madurai is one of the oldest historical cities in India, located in the state of Tamil Nadu. Known as the “Athens of the East,” it is famous for its rich cultural heritage, the Meenakshi Amman Temple, vibrant markets, and thriving urban life.

City Details

  • State: Tamil Nadu
  • District: Madurai
  • Population: ~1.6 million
  • Famous For: Meenakshi Amman Temple
  • Transport Hubs: Madurai Junction Railway Station, MDU Airport
  • Language: Tamil
